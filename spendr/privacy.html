Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Spendr ("the App") is developed and operated by Gorkito ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

Data Collection

Spendr does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your expense data, budget settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage framework (SwiftData).

What We Do Not Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No financial account information (bank accounts, credit cards)
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No cookies or similar technologies

Third-Party Services

Spendr does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, notification scheduling is handled entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to our servers.

Data Export

The App allows you to export your expense data as a CSV file. This export is generated locally on your device. How you share or store that file after export is your responsibility.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time from the App's Settings. Uninstalling the App will also remove all stored data from your device.

Children's Privacy

Spendr does not knowingly collect any information from children. The App does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
